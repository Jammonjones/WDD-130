A Little About Me

Ammon Jones

Background: I Grew up in Utah, in a house of 7. I lived in Kearns for 11 years, and then moved to Sandy when I was 11. I have 3 brothers, and 1 sister. I have always been someone who loves adrenaline, and making things. I love all the adventures that the mountains in Utah make possible.

While a teenage I discovered some of my most favorite hobbies which were mountain biking and snowboarding. I did the bare minimum to get by with A's in school and then would go spend all the time I could with my friends in the mountains doing these hobbies.

I then served a mission in Mozambique and upon returning I met my wife Shannon down at Snow College. After dating for 11 months we were married and now been married for over a year.
